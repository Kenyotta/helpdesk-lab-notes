# Mobile Email Sync / "Cannot Connect to Server" Troubleshooting Guide

## Overview

This guide covers issues where a user is unable to access or sync work email on a mobile device, typically showing errors such as "Cannot connect to server" or missing incoming emails.

---

## Common Symptoms

* Email app shows "Cannot connect to server"
* Emails not syncing on mobile device
* Inbox appears outdated or empty
* Issue only occurs on phone, not on other devices

---

## Initial Checks

### 1. Verify Internet Connectivity

* Confirm mobile device has active internet connection
* Test other apps (browser, messaging, etc.)

### 2. Check Other Devices

* Ask user if email works on:

  * Laptop
  * Webmail (browser-based email)
* If email works elsewhere → issue is device-specific

---

## Likely Causes

* Corrupted mobile app cache/data
* Expired authentication token
* App update causing sync failure
* Local configuration issue on device

---

## Troubleshooting Steps

### Step 1: Isolate the Issue

* Confirm email works on at least one other device
* If yes → no server-side issue

---

### Step 2: Restart App (Basic Fix)

* Close email app completely
* Reopen and test sync

---

### Step 3: Clear or Reset App State (If needed)

If issue persists:

* Uninstall email application
* Reinstall from official app store
* Sign back into account

---

### Step 4: Verify Resolution

* Confirm email sync resumes
* Send/receive test email if necessary

---

## Resolution Summary

Most mobile email sync issues are resolved by reinstalling the application, which clears corrupted cache, outdated tokens, or broken configurations.

---

## When NOT to escalate

Do NOT escalate if:

* Email works on other devices
* Reinstall resolves issue
* No error on server-side systems

---

## When to escalate

Escalate if:

* Email fails on all devices
* Multiple users report same issue
* Server or authentication outage suspected

---

## Key Takeaway

Mobile email issues are most often **local app problems, not server issues**. Always verify cross-device functionality before deeper troubleshooting.
